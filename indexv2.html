Appendix A: FAQ

Where are my files actually stored?

For the community edition, files are stored in your browser's IndexedDB. They never leave your device unless you push to Git.

Does this really work offline?

Yes. The LaTeX compiler is downloaded once (via WebAssembly) and runs entirely on your CPU. You can compile, edit, and export PDFs without any internet connection.

Why support TexWolf?

Building TexWolf helps me sharpen the coding skills needed for modern computational astrophysics. Your patronage covers server costs, books, and the time required to maintain this tool.

How many collaborators I can add in one project?

Unlimited & for free! It uses Peer-to-Peer (P2P) technology.
